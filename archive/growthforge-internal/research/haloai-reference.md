# HaloAI Reference Notes

These notes summarize public website review and WhatsApp intel chat with HaloAI's AI agent. Use as competitive reference only; do not copy claims blindly.

## Public positioning

HaloAI positions itself as:

- AI Agent all-in-one for customer experience,
- close sales,
- create appointments,
- solve customer issues,
- qualify leads,
- enterprise-ready AI Chat + AI Voice + AI CRM.

## Website modules

- AI Chat: multi-channel inbox, natural conversations, human takeover.
- AI Voice: inbound/outbound human-like voice AI and call center workflows.
- AI CRM: ticketing, pipeline, issue tracking, insight.

## Website use cases

- Close Sales.
- Smart Appointment.
- Call Center.
- Issue Customer.
- Qualify Lead.

## Feature claims seen in WhatsApp chat

HaloAI agent introduced itself as Angela and pitched:

- AI sales for automatic 24/7 closing,
- automatic replies,
- QRIS & VA payment,
- recommendation, closing, upselling,
- automatic stock checks,
- automatic ongkir checks,
- CRM & customer tracker,
- Meta Ads integration,
- claimed client revenue increase of 20–40%,
- claimed CS cost reduction up to 90%.

## Pricing behavior

When asked for price, range, or minimum budget, the agent avoided giving public pricing and pushed toward demo meeting.

Observed pattern:

- custom pricing,
- sales-led qualification,
- no public package/range via AI chat,
- asks for business name, social username, contact person, and role.

## WhatsApp number behavior

The agent confirmed HaloAI can use the customer's own WhatsApp business number.

Unknown from chat:

- whether official WhatsApp Business API is used,
- whether QR/session method is supported,
- minimum monthly price,
- exact included features vs add-ons.

## Lessons for GrowthForge

Take:

- outcome-based language,
- AI sales/receptionist framing,
- industry-specific pitch adaptation,
- lead capture before human follow-up,
- customer number support.

Do not take for v1:

- broad all-in-one scope,
- AI Voice,
- CRM platform promise,
- QRIS/VA and payment automation as default,
- stock/ongkir as default,
- ROI claims without case studies,
- fully custom pricing as the only path for SMBs.

## GrowthForge differentiation

GrowthForge should be:

- WA-first,
- UMKM-friendly,
- managed setup,
- package clarity,
- transparent enough for small businesses,
- strict scope wall,
- Basic + Pro first.
