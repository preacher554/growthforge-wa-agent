# Scope Wall Policy

## Purpose

Prevent GrowthForge WA Agent from becoming unlimited custom work hidden inside Basic or Pro packages.

## Standard packages

Basic and Pro are standardized managed packages.

They include configured AI behavior, approved knowledge, simple memory, and handoff rules.

## Not included by default

- SKU/catalog lookup.
- Stock sync.
- Payment verification.
- QRIS/VA.
- Invoice generation.
- Ongkir checks.
- Marketplace integration.
- Meta Ads integration.
- CRM/ERP integration.
- Advanced dashboard.
- Omnichannel.
- AI Voice.
- Unlimited revisions.
- Custom workflow without assessment.

## Client-facing language

```txt
Bisa Kak, tapi itu masuk kebutuhan add-on/custom karena di luar setup standar paket ini. Untuk tahap awal, kami sarankan mulai dari flow utama dulu supaya cepat jalan dan manfaatnya terlihat.
```

## Internal rule

If a feature requires a new external integration, payment flow, catalog sync, dashboard, or custom business logic, it is not Basic/Pro default.
