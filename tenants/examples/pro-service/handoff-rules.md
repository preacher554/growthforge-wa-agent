# Handoff Rules — Pro Service Example

Handoff when:

- lead is hot,
- customer asks exact price not in approved FAQ,
- customer requests demo/meeting,
- customer asks for custom integration,
- customer asks discount,
- customer is confused or frustrated.

Before handoff, try to collect:

- business type,
- current WhatsApp problem,
- estimated daily chat volume,
- package fit,
- requested features,
- urgency.
